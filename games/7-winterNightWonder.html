A winter night wonder

Getting lost in the woods and seeking out a song that you once remembered.

Entry for the Sgap General Art Project.

Unity game
Team of 4 people (including me)






Info!
Play!

Hard coded cutscenes

Coded an introduction cutscene to allow player to select a song at the start of the game. Similarly coded an ending cutscene.


Audio syncing

Created an audio syncing script for the 7 different audio clips in order to make sure they all played in time with each other.


Unity render pipeline optimization

Edited the manifest.json to read the universal render pipeline from a local copied file, and edited the 2d shadow castor scripts for optimisation purposes.


Player movement

Grid based player movement and stars that follow in a trail 1-6 spaces behind the player.

The game was designed so that the player can select a different musician's song at the start. Created one of the songs that can be selected.

General design

Discussed game ideas with lead artist, helped come up with a design idea that satisfied both me and the lead artist (2 musicians joined the project after this).


Level design

Laid out a map area for the game, was described as "Easy to navigate" with the exception of finding the last collectable, which was hidden but could have been less hidden.
